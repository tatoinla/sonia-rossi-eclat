EDITORIAL

The Art of
Edible Luxury

Éclat — meaning "brilliance" and "splendor" in French — is the bold, vibrant expression of Sonia Rossi's creative vision. Where other chocolatiers see confection, Sonia sees canvas.

Each piece is an act of rebellion against the ordinary — a declaration that organic, health-conscious chocolate can be the most visually stunning, most emotionally resonant, and most unforgettable luxury experience in any room.
